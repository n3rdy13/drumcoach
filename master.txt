# Project Handover: Virtual Drum Coach & AI Warm-up Generator

We have successfully bootstrapped a state-of-the-art drumming practice and timing suite called "MetroMe". 

### What We Just Built:
1. **AI Warm-up Generator**: Created an "AI Warm-up" button inside the AI Instructor chat panel. It reads the user's live practice `timingHistory`, calculates average hit offsets (early/late tendencies) and timing jitter, and packages this data to send to Coach Dave's LLM endpoint. It responds with a customized 5-minute warm-up sheet tailored to fix their timing issues!
